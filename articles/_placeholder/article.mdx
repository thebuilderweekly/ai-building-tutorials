# Articles are a work in progress

This is a placeholder.

The Builder Weekly is building out a library of foundation articles that answer specific questions about AI building: what it is, who does it, how the economics work, what trust means in AI systems.

These articles will be the canonical references the publication points to. Every weekly, monthly, and tutorial will link up to the relevant foundation article. Every foundation article will link down to the supporting weeklies and tutorials.

Right now, this placeholder exists only to make sure the infrastructure that renders articles is working. When the first real article ships, this will be replaced.

If you want to contribute an article, the process will open to external contributions soon.
